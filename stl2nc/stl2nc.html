STL to PNG and GCODE conversion tool

This website lets you convert STL files to PNG files for use with Carbide Create Pro, and will also generate Gcode for use directly with Shapeoko CNC machines.   All computation happens local and inside your browser, no information is uploaded or sent over the internet. For large files this can take 10+ seconds before the results show up.
